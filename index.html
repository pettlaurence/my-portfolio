About

I'm a web developer specializing in front-end development, experienced across most stages of the build cycle for dynamic web projects — from clarifying a client's goals to shipping a working page.

I'm also currently sharpening my SEO practice using Google Webmaster Tools, and I bring a technical-support background to every project — comfortable troubleshooting hardware, software, and hosting (cPanel) issues along the way.
